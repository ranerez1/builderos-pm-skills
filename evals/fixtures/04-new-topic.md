# Brazil SMB market — opportunity scan

First-pass research on whether the Brazil SMB segment is worth pursuing as a 2027 expansion target. New topic — no prior knowledge in the vault on Brazil or LATAM as a market.

## Market size

- ~6.5M registered SMBs in Brazil (IBGE 2025).
- ~280K of them use a paid billing/finance SaaS product (estimate, IDC LATAM 2025).
- Stripe Brazil launched 2023, growing ~40% YoY in the SMB cohort.

## Competitive landscape

- Conta Azul (local, ~80K customers, accounting-led).
- Nibo (local, ~40K, finance ops).
- QuickBooks Brazil (Intuit's local edition, smaller share than expected).
- No clear leader in the "billing reconciliation for multi-channel SMB" niche we'd be entering.

## Localization considerations

- PIX (instant payments) handling — non-negotiable for Brazil SMB.
- Boleto bancário integration — still ~25% of B2B transactions.
- Portuguese UI; LGPD compliance for data residency.

## Recommendation

Worth a Q3 2026 deeper-dive: pick 5 prospects from the Stripe Brazil cohort, do customer development calls. Don't commit roadmap until those are done.
